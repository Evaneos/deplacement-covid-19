This service is for Evaneos agencies. It makes it easy to create vouchers as PDF documents for our travelers for the COVID-19 crisis. A voucher is a document issued by a travel agency, under their full responsibility. The agency can use any other system to issue their vouchers.

Once issued, the PDF vouchers must be uploaded to RM or PRO when setting a trip to "Deferred" status. Learn more about the vouchers in the Help Center.
